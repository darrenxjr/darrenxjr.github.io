Project Athena

The product we’ve come up with is called, “Project Athena”, it aims to reduce the number accidents involving PMD users and cyclists. We first target PMD users and cyclists to reinforce them with the proper rules and usage of such devices. As a two-pronged approach, our product then aims to encourage pedestrians to empathize with PMD users and cyclists. Our final goal through this product is to foster mutual understanding between cyclists and non-cyclists so that both parties can use Singapore’s paths and roads in harmony. Although our product aims to work with most of the VR devices, such as Google Cardboard and Samsung Gear VR, it will not work on VR devices that does not require a smartphone or requires positional tracking devices, such as Oculus Rift, HTC Vive and Sony Playstation VR.
